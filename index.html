Patrick LeRoy VanAntwerp

Husband, Father, and Veteran



The following excerpt is from an obituary on ancestry.com:
"Patrick LeRoy Van Antwerp, 58, of Berthoud died of cancer Sept. 26, 2003, at his home. He was born July 11, 1945, in Sioux City, Iowa, to Robert John Van Antwerp and Lillian Warrick Van Antwerp. He graduated from Rancho High School in Las Vegas, Nev. He married Mary Louise Kinch on July 12, 1966, in Mead. He moved from Las Vegas to Loveland in 1966, and he later moved to Berthoud. Mr. Van Antwerp served in the U.S. Air Force from 1965 to 1988 and retired at the rank of master sergeant. He was the crew chief on Air Force 2. After retiring from the service, he worked for the Loveland Fire Department, Gerrity Oil and King Soopers.
